ABOUT THE MAG

Sinθ is an international print-based creative arts magazine made by and for the Sino diaspora. The magazine was founded in March 2016 by Jiaqi Kang, Iris Lang, and Michelle Tay. If you are interested in supporting the magazine financially, please donate here.
